# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

---

##  DO's

1. **Keep your profiles professional** - Use a clear, appropriate profile photo and a bio that reflects your academic and career interests.
2. **Think before you post** - Ask yourself: "Would I be comfortable if my professor, parent, or future employer saw this?" If not, don't post it.
3. **Use social media to network meaningfully**- Connect with seniors, faculty, industry professionals, and peers in your field. Engage with relevant content thoughtfully.
4. **Showcase your work** - Share your projects, certifications, and achievements on LinkedIn and GitHub to build a visible portfolio.
5. **Protect your privacy settings** - Review and update your privacy settings on all platforms. Limit who can see your personal information.
6. **Credit others' work** - If you share someone else's content, always give proper credit and attribution.
7. **Stay informed, not reactive** - Verify information before sharing. Do not spread unverified news or rumours.

---

## DON'Ts

1. **Don't post anything offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate** - Hateful, abusive, or obscene content can damage your reputation permanently and may have legal consequences.
2. **Don't share personal details publicly** - Avoid posting your phone number, home address, ID details, or daily location/schedule publicly.
3. **Don't engage in or ignore cyberbullying** - Do not participate in online harassment. If you witness it, report it and support the person being targeted.
4. **Don't overshare your opinions on controversial topics** - Especially on platforms where your real name and institution are visible, be mindful that your views reflect on you professionally.
5. **Don't share unverified information or forwards** - Spreading misinformation, even unintentionally, can cause harm and harm your credibility.
6. **Don't use college-related platforms for personal venting** - Keep WhatsApp groups, class forums, and college communities focused and respectful.
7. **Don't accept friend/connection requests from strangers** - Be cautious about who you connect with, especially on personal platforms like Instagram and Facebook.
